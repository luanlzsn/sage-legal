## About these documents

Both policies are written based on Sage's actual implementation:

- **Fully on-device.** All AI processing — chat, image understanding, speech recognition, text recognition — runs locally on your iPhone. No content is ever sent to any server.
- **No data collection.** We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal data.
- **No third-party SDKs.** No analytics, no advertising, no tracking.
- **Purchases handled by Apple.** In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We never see your payment information.
- **Open-source models.** If you choose to download an open-source model (Llama, Qwen, etc.), only the model weight files are downloaded from public sources. No user data is sent back.

Effective date: **May 27, 2026**
