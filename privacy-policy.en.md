# Privacy Policy

**Effective May 27, 2026**

Sage is an AI assistant that runs entirely on your iPhone. We respect your privacy — we do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal data. This policy explains how we handle (or, more accurately, how we do not handle) your data.

## 1. We do not collect data

Sage does not collect, upload, or store any form of personal data. All conversations, documents, images, and recordings stay on your device only.

## 2. On-device processing

All AI processing — chat generation, image understanding, speech recognition, text recognition — runs locally on your iPhone. No content is sent to any server.

## 3. Device permissions

Sage may request the following permissions when you use the corresponding features:

- **Camera** — taking photos for cutout, redaction, OCR, etc.
- **Microphone** — recording for meeting transcription and live translation
- **Photo Library** — picking images
- **Speech Recognition** — transcribing your speech on-device

All such data is used only for on-device processing and never leaves your device.

## 4. Model downloads

If you choose to use open-source models (e.g. Llama, Qwen), the app downloads model weight files from public sources such as Hugging Face to your device. The download transmits only the model files; no user data is sent back.

## 5. Purchases handled by Apple

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Sage never sees your payment information. Refund requests are handled by Apple Support.

## 6. No third-party services

Sage does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## 7. Children's privacy

Sage is not targeted at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information. Because the app does not collect any information at all, it is safe for children to use.

## 8. Data security

All local data is protected by the standard iOS sandbox and indirectly by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. You can clear all local data at any time by deleting the app.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We will notify you in-app when this policy is updated. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of the updated terms.

## 10. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the App Store listing.
